The documents on this site include descriptions of abuse of Indigenous children and families, as well as racist language used by boarding school administrators.

Native leaders have called for additional mental health support as Indigenous communities learn more about this history. Currently, the Urban Indian Center of Salt Lake provides outpatient mental health services. Learn more at uicsl.org. And there is a Tribal Resource Tool at tribalresourcetool.org.

Additionally, the National Native American Boarding School Healing Coalition offers an online list of trauma resources.
